# Privacy, Consent & DSARs

## Purpose

Ensure transparent processing, consent management, and data subject rights.

## Controls in place

-  Usercentrics CMP for consent and category-based loading (analytics, support, monitoring)
-  Transparent Notices; cookie categories mapped to services
-  DSAR workflow: verification, export, deletion via Firebase and connected systems

## Evidence & Tooling

-  CMP configuration, DSAR tickets/exports

## Review Cadence & Owner

-  Semi-annual; Owner: Head of Engineering
