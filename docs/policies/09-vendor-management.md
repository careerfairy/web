# Vendor & Data Processing Management

## Purpose

Manage third-party processors and data sharing compliantly.

## Controls in place

-  Maintain up-to-date list of processors and purposes
-  DPAs in place; transfer mechanisms verified where relevant
-  Risk assessments for new vendors (data categories, region, security posture)
-  Annual review of vendor list and data flows

## Evidence & Tooling

-  Vendor inventory, DPAs, risk assessments

## Review Cadence & Owner

-  Annual; Owner: Head of Engineering
