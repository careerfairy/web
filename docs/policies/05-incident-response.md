# Incident Response

## Purpose

Provide a consistent approach to detect, respond, and learn from incidents.

## Controls in place

-  Severity levels and on-call escalation path
-  Detection via Sentry, application logs, uptime checks
-  Containment/eradication steps documented per service (Firebase/Vercel/Stripe)
-  Customer and regulator notifications per legal requirements
-  Post-incident review with action items and owners

## Evidence & Tooling

-  Sentry issues, runbooks, incident tickets

## Review Cadence & Owner

-  After every incident and quarterly; Owner: Head of Engineering
